Your Home’s First Line of Defense

In Florida, a roof is more than just a cover; it’s a shield against tropical storms, intense UV radiation, and heavy humidity. At King Roofing and Sheet Metal, we bring commercial-grade discipline to residential projects. We don't just swap out shingles; we engineer a complete roofing system designed to last.

Residential Roofing Systems

We install top-tier materials from manufacturers like GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed. We help you choose the right material for your budget, HOA requirements, and architectural style.

Architectural Shingles

Cost-effective and versatile. We use dimensional shingles rated for 130mph winds to withstand hurricane season.

Metal Roofing

The ultimate in durability. Standing seam systems reflect heat, lower AC bills, and can last 50+ years.

Clay & Concrete Tile

Classic Florida aesthetic. Superior resistance to rot and insects, perfect for high-end estates.

The King Roofing Process

We know that construction on your home can be stressful. We’ve refined our process to be as non-intrusive as possible.

1
Detailed Inspection: We check not just the surface, but the decking, ventilation, and flashings.
2
Property Protection: We use tarps to protect your landscaping, pool, and driveway during tear-off.
3
Code Compliance: All re-roofs include bringing your deck nailing pattern up to current Florida Building Code.
4
Magnetic Sweep: We run heavy-duty magnets over your yard multiple times to catch stray nails, keeping your family and pets safe.

Dealing with Storm Damage?

If you suspect wind or hail damage, do not wait. Small leaks can rot trusses quickly. We are experienced in documenting damage for insurance claims and providing honest assessments—if it can be repaired, we'll tell you.
