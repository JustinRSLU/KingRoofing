Protecting Your Business Bottom Line

At King Roofing and Sheet Metal, we understand that a commercial roof is a critical asset. Downtime due to leaks affects your operations and profitability. We specialize in low-slope and flat roofing systems designed for the harsh Florida sun and storm season.

Our Commercial Systems

  • TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin): The most popular choice for Florida businesses. It reflects UV rays, lowering cooling costs significantly.
  • Modified Bitumen: Ideal for buildings with high foot traffic or rooftop equipment.
  • Metal Roofing: Standing seam systems for longevity and extreme wind resistance.
  • Roof Coatings: Extend the life of your existing roof without a full tear-off.

Our Commercial Roofing Process

We start every project with an OSHA compliant safety plan. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your tenants or employees. All projects are inspected by a supervisor to ensure manufacturer warranty compliance.
