Modern Roofing for a Growing City

Wesley Chapel is one of the fastest-growing communities in Florida. With high-end developments and sprawling commercial centers, property owners need a roofer who understands modern construction standards. King Roofing delivers precision installation for both tile estates and large-scale commercial flat roofs.

Tile Roofing Specialists

Many homes in Wesley Chapel feature clay or concrete tile roofs. While durable, these systems require specialized maintenance.
We offer:

  • Tile Repairs: Fixing slipped or cracked tiles to prevent leaks.
  • Underlayment Replacement: Lifting the tile to replace worn underlayment (the primary waterproofing layer) and re-installing the tile.

Commercial Roofing for Retail & Medical

From the Wiregrass area to SR-54, we support Wesley Chapel's business infrastructure. Our crews are certified in single-ply TPO systems that offer high solar reflectance—vital for reducing energy costs in large commercial spaces.
