Frequently Asked Questions

Does applying hurt my credit score?

Most of our partners offer a "soft pull" pre-qualification that allows you to see your options without impacting your credit score.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes! The majority of our financing plans have no prepayment penalties, meaning you can pay off the balance whenever you like to save on interest.

Do you offer financing for repairs or just replacements?

Financing is available for projects over a certain threshold (typically $1,000+), so major repairs are often eligible.
