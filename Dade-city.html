Dependable Roofing for Dade City

As the county seat of Pasco County, Dade City represents a blend of commercial activity and agricultural heritage. From the antique shops downtown to the surrounding ranch lands, King Roofing provides durable solutions that withstand the test of time.

Metal Roofing Experts

Given Dade City's rural surroundings, Metal Roofing is a top choice for homeowners and business owners alike. It offers superior fire resistance, longevity, and energy efficiency. We specialize in:

  • Standing Seam Metal: Hidden fasteners for a sleek look and maximum weatherproofing.
  • Ag-Panel / 5V Crimp: Cost-effective solutions for barns, sheds, and agricultural outbuildings.

Commercial Flat Roofs

We provide full maintenance and replacement services for Dade City’s commercial buildings. Whether you have an aging built-up roof or need a modern TPO retro-fit, our team ensures your business stays dry during the summer storm season.
